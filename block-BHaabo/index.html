Why we are doing AltCampus?

AltCampus is an alternative to college that you wished existed. Currently, we are offering an in-house, 6-month long program to train people in quality software development, where they dont have to pay anything until they are hired.

We live in the world of digital skills. These skills are always evolving and so in their demand. Students/Professionals who are not responsive to changes in industry demands, in terms of skills, will have a hard time surviving, well at least flourishing.
